Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 06, 2026

At Secure Dialer, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how your data is handled.

1. Local Data Processing

We believe in data sovereignty. Your contacts, call logs, and personal calling history are never transmitted to our servers. All dialer functions and data processing occur exclusively on your device.

2. Data Usage

Your information is used only to provide core dialer functionality:

  • Contacts: Accessed solely to map phone numbers to names within the app's local interface.
  • Call Logs: Accessed only to display your local call history.
  • Microphone: Used only when you are in an active call to transmit your voice to the party you are calling.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties, including advertisers or analytics providers.

4. Your Control

You have full control over your data. Permissions can be revoked at any time through your device's system settings.
