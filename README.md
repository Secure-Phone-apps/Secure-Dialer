# Secure Dialer

**Secure Dialer** is a lightweight, privacy-first Android dialer designed for users who value performance, simplicity, and data security. Built with a modern Material 3 interface, it strips away the bloat of stock dialers while providing all the essential features you need for daily communication.

---

### 🛡️ Privacy-First & Offline
Your data belongs to you. **Secure Dialer requires no internet permissions.** It operates entirely offline, reading and writing your call logs and contacts directly to your device's local database. No data is ever sent to a server, ensuring your personal information remains strictly on your phone.

---

### ✨ Key Features
*   **Modern Material 3 Design:** A clean, minimal, and aesthetic user interface.
*   **Resource Optimized:** Extremely lightweight on RAM and CPU; designed to minimize cache buildup and device storage usage.
*   **Core Functionality:** Includes a full-featured dialer, call logs, and a built-in contact manager (add/edit/delete).
*   **Privacy Centric:** No internet access, no tracking, no bloatware.
*   **Theme Support:** Seamless switching between Light and Dark themes.

---

### 📱 Getting Started
This app interacts directly with your Android system's call logs and contacts. Upon first launch, please grant the necessary permissions when prompted to allow the dialer to function correctly.
